## REALITY CHECK

**What makes this work:**
- Mint provides real runway ($13-18K net)
- Token flywheel increases effective capture over time
- Enterprise is the 10x lever (way higher margins, way higher revenue)
- Public customers at full price are the volume play
- Multiple revenue streams compound

**What could kill it:**
- If nobody mints (marketing is everything)
- If support costs eat margins (automate HARD)
- If churn is higher than modeled (retention = product quality)
- If you don't accumulate tokens fast enough
- If you spend the mint cash on non-revenue things

**The 20% ops concern:**
Early months, 20% of profit is thin. But costs are covered BEFORE the split. The 20% is reinvestment/salary money. As revenue scales, 20% of $60K/mo profit = $12K/mo. Plus token capture. It compounds.
