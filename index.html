// FAQ

Questions we keep getting.

What makes KIN different from a chatbot? +
KIN isn't a chatbot. It's a companion. Each KIN is purpose-built for your world — your industry, your workflows, your life. It grows with you, lives across your devices, and actually remembers who you are. Chatbots answer questions. KIN shows up.
Do I need to train it? +
Nope. Your KIN arrives already knowing your world. Pick, hatch, go. It adapts to you over time — learning your voice, your preferences, your way of working. But it comes smart from day one.
Can I switch between KIN types? +
Absolutely. Subscription plans unlock multiple KINs. Run MarKIN for growth, FinKIN for budgets, and LawKIN for contracts — all at once.
What's this NFT thing about? +
Your KIN is minted as a compressed NFT on Solana. That means you own it — tradeable, portable, provably yours. Founding KINs from presale get lifetime perks.
Is my data safe? +
Yes. Your conversations stay yours. No training on your data. No sharing. Enterprise plans include private deployment options.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Of course. No contracts. No commitments. No "please don't go" emails. Cancel with one click.
What if my industry isn't listed? +
